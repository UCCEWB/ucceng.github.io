News

Slack - December 18, 2016 11:35am

The Upper Canada College Engineers Without Borders club is now using Slack. Slack has many features that will allow us to stay organized and to be more productive. Using Slack will also mean that it can reduce the Gmail chatter.

How to join:

1. You should receive an email from Michael or Simon asking you to join the UCC Engineers Without Borders organization.
Note: If you do not receive an invitation from Michael or Simon, please email them.
2. Click the link and follow the prompts to create an account.
3. You should automatically be added into the announcements and random group.

Downloads:

There is a Slack app for almost every platform. Click here to get the Slack download for your OS. We strongly recommend you download the macOS/OS X version for your Mac here.

First Club Meeting - December 13, 2016

We will be holding our first club meeting on Tuesday December 13, 2016 in room 127. Come on out and see what the EWB club is all about. If you are unable to make it and would like more information about the club, please send an email to the club heads.

Club Heads Announced - December 1, 2016

Welcome to the Engineers Without Borders club. There will be five club heads for this year, Michael Bateman, Simon Guo, Shaan Hooey, Geoffrey Jiang and Isaac Ng. If you would like to get in touch, please email them on the contact page.

Hello, world! - December 1, 2016

Welcome to the Engineers Without Borders Club. The club will be officially starting in January after the winter clubs fair. Stay tuned for news on the club heads.
